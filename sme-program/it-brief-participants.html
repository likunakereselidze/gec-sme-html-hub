Required Features

Password login screen

Before seeing any data, the user must enter a password. Session stays active until the browser is closed.

Participants table

Full list of all submitted applications with columns: #, name, company, sector, employees, phone, email, info source, date.

Search and filter

Search by name, company or email. Filter by business sector and number of employees.

Detail view per applicant

Click any row to open a panel showing all fields including the full motivation/growth potential text.

CSV export

One-click button to download all submissions as a CSV file, compatible with Excel and Google Sheets.

Stats summary bar

Shows total applications, submissions today, and most common sector at the top of the page.
